India is one of the most visited tourist destinations in Asia. India offers a variety of sights to see and things to do. It is bordered by the Himalayan ranges in the north and the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean on three sides.

India is a wonderful country because of its picturesque backwaters, hill towns, and landscapes. Tourists are drawn to historical sites, beaches, places of worship, hill resorts, and other attractions. Every region has its own distinctive handicraft, festivals, traditional dances, music, and population - making it a top choice!
